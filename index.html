01 // LOYALTY
Digital Loyalty

DigiCard ↗

DigiCard reimagines the paper loyalty card as a living digital wallet. Instead of a flimsy punch card that gets lost in a junk drawer, customers collect stamps and track rewards straight from their phone, while businesses get a simple dashboard to issue cards, log visits, and watch loyalty grow in real time. Built around the idea that repeat customers are a business's most valuable asset, DigiCard strips away the friction of physical cards and paper stamps, replacing them with a clean, tap-to-stamp system that works for cafes, salons, gyms, and anywhere else a tenth visit deserves a reward.

02 // MESSAGING
Messaging

DigiMessage ↗

DigiMessage is a fast, no-nonsense messaging system built for real-time conversation without the bloat. It focuses on the essentials — instant delivery, clean threads, and a distraction-free interface — so conversations feel immediate instead of buried under notifications and clutter. Under the hood it's built to handle live message delivery smoothly, making it a solid foundation for anything from casual group chats to a lightweight team communication tool. Simple in, simple out: DigiMessage is messaging stripped back to what actually matters.

03 // MEMORY
Location Memories

Echo Archive ↗

Echo Archive turns any location into a time capsule of anonymous memories. Drop a pin anywhere and read what strangers have left behind about that exact spot — a first date, a childhood memory, a moment that mattered to someone who was once standing right there. Anyone can leave their own memory tied to a place, building up a living, anonymous map of human experience layered over the physical world. It's less a map for getting somewhere and more a map for understanding somewhere — because every place has a story.

04 // CLINICAL
Dental Practice Tool

OraDex ↗

OraDex is a modern practice management tool built specifically for dental clinics that need patient information fast. Rather than digging through charts mid-appointment, dental staff can run structured flowcharts that surface the right patient history, treatment notes, and next steps in just a few taps — built for the pace of a real clinic, not a slow back-office system. It's designed to sit between the front desk and the chair, cutting down the time spent hunting for information so practices can spend more of it actually treating patients.
